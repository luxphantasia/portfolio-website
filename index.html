About

I'm a Senior 3D Generalist & Character Artist based in Georgia, USA. I've been developing and creating 3D art since December 2021, specializing in stylized character work, UGC creation, and VTuber models.

Currently working as Lead Senior 3D + Head of Character Art at Anime Overload, and commission-based with Combat Warriors. Both games pull billions of plays — I operate in fully professional studio pipelines.

I prefer working behind the scenes. No public credit needed — I let the work speak for itself. My focus is on quality, reliability, and building long-term partnerships with creators and studios who value both.
